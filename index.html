Types of Business Insurance Coverage

At SD Insurance, we offer a range of business insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Property Insurance: Covers physical damage to your business property.
  • Liability Insurance: Protects against legal liabilities for injury or damage to third parties.
  • Business Interruption Insurance: Covers loss of income due to insured perils.
  • Workers' Compensation Insurance: Covers medical expenses and lost wages for injured employees.
